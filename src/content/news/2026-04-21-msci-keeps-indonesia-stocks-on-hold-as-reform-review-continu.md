## Summary
MSCI maintains Indonesia's "under review" status for its equity market classification, deferring any upgrade decision while IDX continues implementing transparency and accessibility reforms.

## Key Points
• MSCI's April 2026 review: Indonesia remains on watchlist, upgrade deferred
• Primary concerns: foreign investor registration (KSEI), short-selling framework, T+2 settlement
• IDX has accelerated reforms including real-time foreign ownership disclosure and improved corporate actions transparency
• Next review cycle: August 2026 — potential upgrade window if reforms complete
