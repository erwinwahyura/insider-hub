## Summary
Indonesian superapp GoTo (Gojek + Tokopedia) reported its first-ever quarterly net profit on April 28, driven by rapid fintech expansion and "premium" segment focus.

## Key Points
• GoTo achieved first quarterly net profit since merger — milestone for Indonesian tech sector
• Fintech business (GoPay, lending) drove turnaround alongside ride-hailing and e-commerce
• Company attributes success to premium segment strategy and AI implementation
• Signals improving profitability in Indonesian digital economy after years of losses
