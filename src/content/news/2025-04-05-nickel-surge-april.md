## Summary

Indonesia's Harga Mineral Acuan (HMA) nickel for April 2025 surged to $16,126.33 per ton - up from $15,534.62 in March (+3.8% MoM). APNI (Indonesian Nickel Miners Association) announced significant price increase for first period of April.
