## Summary
Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) accelerates market transparency reforms and officially denies MSCI downgrade rumors, aiming to maintain Emerging Market status and boost foreign investor confidence.

• **Official denial**: BEI explicitly rejects downgrade rumors, reaffirming commitment to EM status
• **Foreign confidence**: Reforms target institutional investor requirements for market accessibility
