## Summary

Surging oil prices driven by the Iran war are pushing Japan toward potential yen intervention as the currency weakens and import costs rise, creating ripple effects across Asian markets.

## Key Points

• **Oil Price Surge**: Iran war driving significant oil price increases, impacting energy-importing nations
• **Japan Yen Intervention**: Japanese authorities considering currency intervention to support weakening yen
• **Asian Market Impact**: Energy price volatility affecting regional economies and central bank policies
