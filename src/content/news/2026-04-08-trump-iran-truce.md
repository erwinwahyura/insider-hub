## Summary
President Trump announced a two-week suspension of military action against Iran following Pakistan-mediated discussions, with negotiations set to begin Friday in Islamabad contingent on Iran reopening the Strait of Hormuz.

## Key Points
• **Truce Declared:** 14-day suspension of US strikes against Iran
• **Pakistan Mediation:** Trump cites "10-point proposal" from Iran as workable basis
• **Condition:** Iran must reopen Strait of Hormuz for talks to proceed
• **Timeline:** Negotiations start Friday in Islamabad

## Portfolio Impact
🟢 **Positive for Energy/Geothermal Plays**

- **Coal/ADRO:** Alternative energy sources less critical if oil supply routes secure
