## Summary
The Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) responds to MSCI's decision to maintain its freeze on Indonesian stocks from certain indices, addressing market access and foreign investor concerns.

## Key Points
• MSCI maintains freeze on Indonesian stocks in key indices
• IDX issues official response to the decision
• Foreign investor access and market structure under scrutiny
• Potential impact on passive fund flows into Indonesia
• IDX working to address MSCI concerns

## Portfolio Impact
🟡 **Neutral/Watch** — MSCI freeze affects passive fund flows but doesn't directly impact your individual holdings. ITMG, PTPS, ESSA, PGEO fundamentals remain unchanged. Monitor for any resolution that could boost foreign inflows to IDX overall.
