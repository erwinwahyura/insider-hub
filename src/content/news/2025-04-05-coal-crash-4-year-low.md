## Summary

Newcastle coal prices have collapsed to $93.75 per ton - the lowest in four years. Indonesia's coal exports dropped 6.43% to 160M tonnes (Jan-Apr 2025) amid weak China/India demand. H1 2025 export value plunged ~21% YoY.

## Key Points

- **Newcastle coal: $93.75/ton** — 4-year low, down from $125+ in early 2025
- **Indonesia exports** dropped 6.43% to 160M tonnes (Jan-Apr 2025)
- **Export value crashed** ~21% YoY in H1-2025 as China & India cut imports
- **Kiwoom Securities outlook**: Bearish H2-2025 — export demand further weakening
