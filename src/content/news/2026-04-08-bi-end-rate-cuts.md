## Summary
Bank Indonesia signals it may pause or end its rate-cutting cycle as global geopolitical tensions and market volatility increase, prioritizing rupiah stability over growth stimulus.

## Key Points
• **Policy Shift:** BI hints at end to easing cycle after multiple rate cuts in 2024-2025
• **Global Factors:** US-Iran tensions, trade uncertainty driving caution
• **Rupiah Priority:** Currency stability takes precedence over growth support
• **Next Meeting:** BI Board of Governors meeting scheduled 23-24 April 2026

## Portfolio Impact
🟡 **Neutral/Macro Watch**

**For your positions:**
- **PTPS, PGEO, ESSA:** No immediate earnings impact from rate pause
- **USD/IDR:** Potential rupiah support if BI holds firm — watch 15,800-16,200 range
- **Market Sentiment:** Less liquidity-driven upside, focus shifts to fundamentals

**Outlook:** If BI holds at 6.25%, expect range-bound market until earnings clarity. B50 implementation and commodity prices now drive returns more than monetary policy.
