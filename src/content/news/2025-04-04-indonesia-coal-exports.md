## Summary

Indonesia's coal exports surged 12% MoM to 46.5M tonnes in March, driven by Indian heat wave demand and China manufacturing recovery. Newcastle coal prices hit $128/t (+8% from Feb), with HBA benchmark at $126.50/t. Ministry may relax DMO rules to allow more exports.

## Key Points

- Coal exports +12% MoM to 46.5M tonnes (India heat wave + China recovery)
- Coal prices: Newcastle $128/t, HBA $126.50/t — both up ~8% from Feb
- DMO rules may be relaxed → more export volume allowed
- ADRO & ITMG already up 3-4% this week; strong Q1 shipments expected
