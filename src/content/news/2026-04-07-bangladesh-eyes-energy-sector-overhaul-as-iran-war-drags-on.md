## Summary
Bangladesh is planning a comprehensive energy sector overhaul as the prolonged Iran war continues to disrupt global energy supplies and create supply chain uncertainties across Asia.

• Regional energy security concerns mounting as Middle East conflict extends
• LNG and coal import dependencies creating vulnerability for South Asian economies
• Energy transition acceleration likely as countries seek supply diversification
• Infrastructure investment needs rising for alternative energy sources
